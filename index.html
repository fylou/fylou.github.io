[Profile] I am an Associate Professor (H&J Youth Chair Professor) in the Department of Computer Science, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, Taiwan, where I previously served as an Assistant Professor from August 2023 to January 2025. Meanwhile, I am the Principal Investigator of the Network Science and Graph Learning Lab and a Member of the Communication Service/Software Lab.
I received my B.E. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from Xidian University, Xi'an, China, in 2008, and my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, in 2017. From 2017 to 2023, I held research and academic positions at City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong; Lingnan University, Hong Kong; and Osaka University, Osaka, Japan.
My current research interests include Network Science & Engineering, Machine & Graph Learning, and Optimization.

[Join Us!] I am recruiting self-motivated Ph.D. and M.Sc. students interested in conducting RESEARCH to solve meaningful problems in communication network systems, such as using machine learning, evolutionary computing, and other computational methods to optimize Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) deployment.
