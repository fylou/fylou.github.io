[Profile] I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), Taiwan. I received the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, in 2017, under the supervision of Professor Shiu Yin Yuen. I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with the Centre for Complexity and Complex Networks and the Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, from 2017 to 2021, under the supervision of Professor Guanrong Chen, and a Research Assistant Professor with the Department of Computing and Decision Sciences, Lingnan University, Hong Kong, form 2021 to 2022. From 2022 to 2023, I worked as a Specially Appointed Researcher with the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology, Osaka University, Japan. My current research interests include Network Science, Machine Learning, Graph Learning, and Evolutionary Computation.

[Join Us!] I am looking for M.Sc. and Ph.D. students at the Department of Computer Science, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. If you are interested in utilizing Machine Learning, Graph (Representation) Learning, or Evolutionary Computing to solve meaningful problems in Network Science, please don't hesitate to email me yanglou[at]cs.nycu.edu.tw.
