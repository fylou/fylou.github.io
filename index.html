[ Profile ] I am currently a Research Assistant Professor with the Department of Computing and Decision Sciences, Lingnan University. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong in 2017, under the supervision of Professor Shiu Yin Yuen. From 2017 to 2021, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with the Centre for Chaos and Complex Networks and the Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Professor Guanrong Chen. My current research interests include complex networks, evolutionary computation, and machine learning.
