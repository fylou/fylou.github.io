[Profile] I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Electronic Engineering (now Electrical Engineering), City University of Hong Kong in 2017, where I have been a Postdoctoral Research Fellow since then. Before that I received my B.E. and M.S. degrees from Xidian University and Ningbo University, respectively. My current research interests include complex networks, evolutionary computation, and machine learning.
