About Me

I received the B.E. degree from Xidian University, China, in 2008, the M.S. degree from Ningbo University, China, in 2012, and the Ph.D. degree from Department of Electrical Engineering (was Electronic Engineering), City University of Hong Kong in 2017, where I have been a Postdoctoral Research Fellow since then.

My current research interests include complex networks, evolutionary computation, and machine learning.
