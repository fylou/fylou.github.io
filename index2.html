Welcome to Felix's Homepage


[Profile] I received the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong in 2017, under the supervision of Professor Shiu Yin Yuen. I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with the Centre for Chaos and Complex Networks and the Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, from 2017 to 2021, under the supervision of Professor Guanrong Chen, and a Research Assistant Professor with the Department of Computing and Decision Sciences, Lingnan University, Hong Kong form 2021 to 2022. I am a visiting Associate Professor with the College of Computer Science, Sichuan Normal University, China, and have been appointed a Specially Appointed Researcher with the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology, Osaka University, Japan. My current research interests include machine learning, network science, and optimization.
