[Profile] I am an Associate Professor (H&J Youth Chair Professor) in the Department of Computer Science, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, where I was previously an Assistant Professor from August 2023 to January 2025.

I received my B.E. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from Xidian University, China, in 2008, and my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, in 2017. From 2017 to 2023, I held research and academic positions at City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, Lingnan University, Hong Kong, and Osaka University, Japan.

My current research interests include Network Science, Graph Learning, and Optimization.
